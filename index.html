How to Play BrainMatch

  • 🎯 Match all the pairs of cards.
  • 🂿 Click two cards to flip them.
  • 🔁 If they match, they stay flipped. If not, they flip back.
  • 🧠 Use your memory to remember card positions.
  • ⏱ Timer starts with your first move.
  • 🎮 Complete the game quickly with few mistakes.
  • 🔄 Click "Reset Game" to start over.
  • 🔊 Use the sound button to toggle audio.

Good luck and have fun!
